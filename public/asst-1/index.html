History of the University of Maryland

The University of Maryland System involves many campuses and spans the entire state. Here are some details about the more common campuses.

  1. University of Maryland College Park

    Founded by Charles Benedict Calvert

    • Established in October of 1859.
    • Current President is Darryll Pines.
    • Offers 90 majors.

  2. University of Maryland, Baltimore County

    Founded as a result of Maryland governor Theodor McKeldin's issue declaring "Needs of Higher Educatin in Maryland".

    • Established in 1966.
    • Current President is Freeman A. Hrabowski III.
    • Offers 42 majors.

  3. University of Maryland, Global Campus

    Founded as a result of an outgrowth of the evening program for adults at the University of Maryland.

    • Established in 1947.
    • Current President is Javier Miyares.
    • Offers 30 majors.
